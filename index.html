About Us

Founded by brothers Ashok and Anand, the Quadros group of companies commenced panel processing activities in 1995. We set up Multiflex, an entirely automated Computerised Numerical Control factory in 2000. Today, we operate state of the art factories for panel processing and a facility dedicated to offering the most advanced finishes available today.
A Multiflex product is of impeccable quality, fabricated using the best possible manufacturing processes whilst keeping longevity & aesthetics in mind.
